# Soundia 
### *Spatial White Noise for Immersive Focus*

**Soundia** is a spatial audio application designed to transform a quiet, isolating room into a "lively" and productive environment. Built for the **2026 Swift Student Challenge**, it moves beyond flat, static white noise by using 3D soundscapes to help students and remote workers maintain a deep flow state without feeling alone in their space.

---

## 🌟 Features
* **Spatial Audio Engine:** Uses 3D positioning to make sounds feel like they are physically around you, not just "inside" your headphones.
* **Custom Sound Mixing:** Layer multiple high-fidelity sounds—from gentle rainfall to a bustling cafe—to create your perfect atmosphere.
* **Minimalist UI:** A clean, high-contrast interface designed in SwiftUI that supports **Dynamic Type** for accessibility.
* **No Interruptions:** An ad-free, seamless experience focused entirely on user concentration.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
* **SwiftUI:** For a responsive, declarative user interface that scales across devices.
* **AVFoundation (AVAudioEngine):** The core of the app, utilizing `AVAudioEnvironmentNode` to place audio sources in a 3D coordinate system.
* **Spatial Audio:** Implemented to provide a sense of presence and depth, effectively reducing "listener fatigue."
* **SF Symbols:** Integrated for intuitive, system-consistent iconography.

---

## 🎨 Inspiration
The project was born from the feeling of "empty room" syndrome during late-night study sessions. I realized that white noise shouldn't just be a wall of sound—it should be an environment. **Soundia** bridges that gap by making digital sound feel like a natural part of your physical surroundings, making the atmosphere feel more alive.
